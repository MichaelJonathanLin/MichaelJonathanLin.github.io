Solidworks Experience

I have an excellent understanding of Solidworks and have used Solidworks for various projects.

Ballast Box

As a part of the Midnight Sun Solar Car team, I designed their Ballast Box with the advice of the mechanical leads. The box had to be lightweight, compact and strong enough to hold 60kg of shotgun ballast moving at high speeds within the car. The regulations for the box can be found on the American Solar Challenge website. After many hours of work and four redesigns, the final box was sent out in a water jet order, and will soon be placed in the car.

To design the box I used 14 gauge aluminum with thhe Solidworks Sheet Metal Tool. To make the box easy to manufacture and ship, the box was separated into 3 sheets, a front, a middle and a back. I mirrored the edge flanges and slot patterns about a center plane. The box is fastened with 1/8th inch rivets and is mounted to the chassis using trick tabs.
